Frequently Asked Questions
What is INGRES ChatBot?
INGRES ChatBot is India’s 1st AI-powered chatbot for employee assistance, helping you with HR queries, leave management, and more.
How do I reset my password?
Go to Settings > Change Password, enter your current and new password, and click Save Changes.
Can I change the language of the chatbot?
Yes, you can select your preferred language in the Settings page under the Language section.
How do I view my chat history?
Click on the "Chat History" option in the sidebar to view all your previous conversations.
Who can I contact for support?
You can use the "Contact Support" quick action on the home page or visit the Contact & Support page for more details.
